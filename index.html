Why Coaching

You're smart enough to figure it out yourself.
So why haven't you?

Because the problem isn't information. You've read the books, made the pros and cons list, had 14 coffee chats. The problem is that you can't see your own patterns — the hidden commitments keeping you in place, the stories about your career that are out of date, the gap between what you say you want and what you're actually doing about it.

That's what coaching solves. Not by giving you answers, but by helping you see clearly enough to find your own — and then holding you accountable to acting on them.

Coaching is not therapy, not consulting, not mentorship. It's a structured partnership focused on getting you from where you are to where you want to be.

≠
Not Therapy
Therapy looks backward to heal. Coaching looks forward to build. I'm not treating anything — I'm helping you make better decisions about what's next.

Not Consulting
